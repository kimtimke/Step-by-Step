About me

The Beginning

1981

Born and raised on the Namibian coast, Hans-Werner matriculated at the Swakopmund Secondary School in 1981. He then completed a B.Comm at UCT as well as the postgraduate CTA (Certifcate in the Theory of Accounting). He passed the Board Exam in 1987 and when he completed his articles with PWC (then Coopers & Lybrand) in 1989 in Cape Town he was a fully qualifed Chartered Accountant.
