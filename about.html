About


Hans-Werner Timke is a seasoned professional with extensive experience in finance, management, and entrepreneurship. He is a qualified Chartered Accountant with a background in diverse industries, including dental, oil & gas, fishing, and freight & logistics. With a track record of driving growth and innovation, Hans-Werner has developed concepts like Step-By-Step and WalkiTa to empower individuals and organizations. He brings a wealth of expertise in business systems implementation, financial management, and strategic leadership. Committed to continuous improvement, Hans-Werner is passionate about helping others reach their full potential.

Professional Journey and Personal Milestones"

Professional Journey

1991 - Present

Hans-Werner embarked on a successful career in finance and general management. He held senior positions in industries such as mining, office products and travel & hospitality services. His roles involved leading and improving business systems, managing service-based and trading companies and exploring freight and marine services opportunities.

Entrepreneurial Ventures

2017-2019

Hans-Werner's entrepreneurial spirit led him to establish his own import & export company, importing Namibian products to Germany, amongst others the well-known Windhoek Lager beer.

Step-by-Step and WalkiTa Concepts

2020-Present

Inspired by his personal journey, Hans-Werner developed the Step-By-Step and WalkiTa concepts. These concepts combine his passion for personal and professional growth with the need for exercise and mental stimulation. They aim to provide a refreshing approach to training and skill development by integrating outdoor activities and the exploration of new perspectives.

Family Life

Hans-Werner married his wife Christine in the mid-nineties. Christine brought along a then 17 year old daughter Tina. Two further children, Benjamin and Julia were born to the marriage. Benjamin pursued a career as a pilot, while Julia studied event management. Both children have achieved personal and professional milestones, with Benjamin marrying Kim Seebach in 2022, and Julia working as a senior tour consultant and recently got engaged to Siggi Punzul.

Personal Challenge and Growth

2015

In 2015, Hans-Werner was diagnosed with Parkinson's disease, a neurodegenerative disorder affecting movement. Despite the challenges, he remains determined and has utilized his experience to develop innovative concepts and approaches that positively impact individuals and businesses.
