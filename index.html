Who is Step-by-Step Business Solutions?

STEP BY STEP Business Solutions (SBS) is about taking one step at a time to enhance skills which in turn support improved business performance. Being able to “take steps“ means we can move forward, explore new opportunities, grow people and profits.

The founder of SBS, Hans-Werner Timke is a CA who has played a significant role in shaping and grwoing Namibian Business in his role of Managing Director of various leading companies. He brings a wealth of business knowledge, experience, accumen and fresh insight into changing business processes, people management, leadership and performance enhancement.
