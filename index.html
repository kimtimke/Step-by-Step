Step-by-Step Business Solutions (Namibia)

Welcome to SBS (Step-By-Step) Business Solutions (Namibia), a portfolio of business solutions dedicated to enhancing Namibian business performance through skills development. Our primary objective is to uplift Namibian leadership skills by mitigating the risk of unawareness and fostering improved business understanding and strategy planning. We also prioritize the concept of WalkiTa, which involves engaging in "walk and talk" sessions with clear objectives and expected outcomes, enabling the vital practice of pausing and reflecting. As a start-up on our journey, we invite you to join us in taking step-by-step strides towards extraordinary performance.

Formal Programs

The Formal Program offered by SBS Business Solutions includes structured and comprehensive training programs designed to address specific needs and challenges faced by corporate managers. These programs are tailored to provide individualized support and guidance, helping managers improve their skills and enhance business performance.

Ad-hoc-Solutions

SBS Business Solutions also offers Ad-hoc Solutions, which are flexible and tailored services that can be utilized on a project basis or as needed. These solutions cater to both individual and group requirements, providing targeted support in various areas of business improvement and strategy development.
