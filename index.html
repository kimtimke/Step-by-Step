Step-by-Step Business Solutions

SBS („Step-By-Step“) Business Solutions (Namibia) is a portfolio of business solutions developed by Hans – Werner Timke with the objective to enhance Namibian business performance through skills development. We seek to uplift Namibian leadership skills by reducing the risk of them „not knowing what they don‘t know“. Improved business understanding and strategy planning are also objectives.

What is a WalkiTa?

STEP BY STEP Business Solutions (SBS) is about taking one step at a time to enhance skills which in turn support improved business performance. Being able to “take steps“ means we can move forward, explore new opportunities, grow people and profits.
