Concept


What is the WalkiTa concept about?

  • A Walkita is a 2-3 hour Walk & Talk session outdoors with clear objectives and outcomes.

  • It provides a refreshing, relaxed, and focused environment to think about improving business processes.

  • Participants can choose the topics they want to discuss, such as challenges, opportunities, financial performance, customers, and strategy.

  • Multiple WalkiTa's are organized during the intervention, starting with introductory topics and progressing to specific ones.

  • Walkitas can include "THE difficult questions" about business performance and future profitability.

  • Fun elements like bag pick-ups or photo competitions may be included.

  • The discussion outcomes are summarized in a one or two-page "OUTPUT Sheet" for action planning.
