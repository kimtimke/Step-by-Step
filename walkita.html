What is the WalkiTa

  • Literally a 2 - 3 hour Walk & Talk session in the outdoors with clear objectives and outcomes.
  • As the concept is an outdoors walk, a WalkiTa creates a refreshing, relaxed and yet focused setting to think about business processes and areas for improvement.
  • Not only is walking good exercise, it also allows for a mental step-by step process to THINK and ask „what-if“ I take a step in a different direction?
  • Every step opens up a new perspective that lead to tangible action plans.
  • Participant should talk about the topics that he/she feels are important i.e (Challenges/ Opportunities/ Financial performance/Customers/ Strategy etc.)
  • Very brief info on “personal happiness“ as it may impact the 90 Day program.
  • Leadership questions, People Management, Business Unit targets and Vision.
  • Business Environmental Factors.
  • Key relationships impacting business.
  • “THE difficult questions“... BU performance if good why not great, if Breakeven why not profit and if red numbers why not at least breakeven?
  • A WalkiTa INCLUDES a bag pick-up info, an assesment of the participant, selfie pictures and refreshments.
