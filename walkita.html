Concept


A Refreshing Path to Business Improvement


A Walkita is a unique and invigorating 2-3 hour Walk & Talk session designed to achieve specific objectives and outcomes. Stepping outside the confines of traditional office settings, Walkitas offer a refreshing and relaxed yet focused environment to contemplate business processes and areas for improvement.

Why WalkiTa?

At Walkita, we believe that a change of scenery can inspire fresh perspectives. By immersing participants in the outdoors, we create a setting that encourages free-flowing conversations and fosters creativity. Depending on the program, participants can choose to discuss challenges, opportunities, financial performance, customers, strategy, and more.

The WalkiTa Experience

During our interventions, multiple Walkitas are organized, each with clear objectives and outcomes. The initial session serves as an introduction and an opportunity to get acquainted with the topics at hand. Subsequent sessions delve into specific subjects, and we may even set up a whiteboard on the beach to brainstorm and visualize ideas.

Embracing the Difficult Questions:

WalkiTas aren't afraid to address the tough questions that lead to growth. Whether your business performance is good, break-even, or in the red, we'll explore how to make it better. For SMEs, we focus on the ability to make money and plan for future success.

Fun and Purposeful Elements:

Our WalkiTas aren't all serious; we also know how to incorporate fun factors like bag pick-ups or engaging photo competitions. The relaxed yet purposeful setting fosters a positive environment where participants can thrive.

Are you ready to step outside the ordinary and embark on a transformative journey?
oin us for a WalkiTa and experience the power of the great outdoors in stimulating business improvement and personal growth.
