Formal Programs


These Formal Programs are designed to provide structured guidance, practical tools, and expert support to help managers and organizations achieve their goals. Whether it's enhancing business performance, developing specific skills, or optimizing operations, SBS Business Solutions offers programs that cater to the unique needs of each participant. These Formal Programs equip participants with the knowledge, skills, and strategies needed to achieve extraordinary results and enhance business performance.

90 Day Business Improvement Program

Target Audience: Corporate Managers (Individual)

Duration: 90 days

Description: A comprehensive program designed to enhance business performance and drive improvement in corporate settings. Through personalized coaching, strategic analysis, and practical tools, participants will gain the skills and insights needed to achieve extraordinary results.

30 Day Health-check

Target Audience: SMEs

Duration: 30 days

Description: This program offers a thorough assessment of SMEs, focusing on identifying areas for improvement and implementing effective strategies. Participants will receive expert guidance, practical recommendations, and tailored action plans to enhance their business operations and achieve sustainable growth.

Modular Training Programs

Target Audience: Corporate Managers (e.g., IT, Sales Managers, etc.)

Duration: Customizable based on modules

Description: Tailored training programs that focus on specific functional areas within organizations. Participants can choose from a range of modules such as IT management, sales strategies, leadership development, and more. These modular programs provide targeted knowledge and skills to address specific managerial challenges.

Ad-Hoc-Solutions


The Ad-Hoc Solutions provided by SBS Business Solutions offer flexible and tailored services for specific business needs. Services include Business Analysis for insights, Strategy Development for effective planning, and Idea/Prospect/Business Plan Evaluation for refining ideas and plans. These solutions drive business improvement and deliver actionable recommendations.

Business Analysis

Target Audience: Corporate Managers (Group or Individual)

Duration: Subject to scope

Description: This ad-hoc service provides in-depth business analysis to identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. Through data-driven insights and expert guidance, organizations can make informed decisions, optimize processes, and drive business improvement.

Strategy Facilitation and Development

Target Audience: Group to Business Entity

Duration: Subject to scope

Description: In this collaborative and interactive session, Hans-Werner facilitates the development of strategic plans using the Strategic Triangle Method (STM). By aligning vision, mission, and goals, organizations can create a roadmap for success and make strategic decisions with confidence.

Idea/Prospect/Business Plan Evaluation

Target Audience: Group to Individual

Duration: Subject to scope

Description: This service offers a comprehensive evaluation of business ideas, prospects, or existing business plans. Through expert analysis and feedback, participants can refine their concepts, identify potential risks, and develop robust business plans to increase the chances of success.
